Transparent Electrowetting on dielectric with liquid electrodes (LEWOD)

This project focused on developing a transparent, flexible microfluidic platform for precise manipulation of droplets using electrowetting-on-dielectric (EWOD) technology. By designing and fabricating liquid electrodes instead of conventional solid electrodes, we created a device that combines high optical transparency with reliable droplet control at low voltages.
