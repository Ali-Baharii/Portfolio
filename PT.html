Digital microfluidic coagulometer

This project presents the design and fabrication of a portable, point-of-care device for blood coagulation testing, developed in response to the clinical need for rapid and accessible diagnostics. By electrowetting technology, the device uses precise manipulation of micro-droplets, to accurately measure blood's clotting times. The system is engineered to be compact and user-friendly, offering reliable results in under a minute and significantly reducing the need for laboratory visits, especially for patients on anticoagulant therapy.
